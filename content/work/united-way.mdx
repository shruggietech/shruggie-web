## The Challenge

United Way of Anderson County needed a website that fully represented their mission and services while aligning with updated global United Way brand guidelines. Their existing site was outdated and didn't reflect the scope of what the organization actually does for the community.

## What We Did

We rebuilt the site from the ground up on WordPress.com Business Plan with a staging environment for safe testing before each go-live. Every page was redesigned to align with updated United Way brand guidelines. We established a formal content architecture, handled content creation, configured DNS, and set up email routing. Design and content were consistently delivered with minimal revisions required.

## Key Outcomes

Fully redesigned website aligned with current United Way brand guidelines. Staging-to-production workflow for safe, testable updates. Ongoing monthly maintenance covering core, plugin, and theme updates, content changes, and technical support. Client owns all hosting credentials and content.
